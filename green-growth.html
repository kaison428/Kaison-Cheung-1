Engineering Economics and Decision Making: Green Growth Paper

The green growth paper is one of the written assignments for the Engineering Economics and Decision Making course. This course provides an overview of engineering economics and introduces some fundamental concepts such as the time value of money and inflation, which allow us to evaluate various projects and make fair decisions systematically.

Since there has been rising concerns for the environment and the sustainability of our economy, this paper aims to expand our understanding in Green Growth and how it can be incorporated in engineering economic analysis. To fully understand Green Growth, different Green Growth indicators, the roles of engineers in Green Growth and the appropriateness of assigning monetary values to natural capitals are discussed.

From this assignment, I have developed the skills to communicate complex ideas effectively and professionally. Effective written communication is essential in the engineering industry, especially when collaborating with other professionals and stakeholders in large-scale projects. In addition, being able to research and understand new concepts and information is another important skill in the workplace.
